Linda's World Quiz

Some geographic mini-games

Find The Country

Find the country with the name displayed in the upper right corner by clicking or tapping on the map. The country is highlighted green if you clicked the correct country.

Find The City

Find the city with the name displayed in the upper right corner by clicking or tapping on the map. The city is highlighted green if you clicked the correct city.

Find The City (hard)

Try to locate the the city by placing the marker on the map. After you click "Solve", the distance to the real location of the city is displayed.
